About

My hobby is drawing new things and learning from them. Everyone should learn drawing as drawing new things is full of fun. I draw things and sceneries very well. Drawing is a form of visual art in which an artist uses instruments to mark paper or other two-dimensional surface. Drawing instruments include graphite pencils, pen and ink, various kinds of paints, inked brushes, colored pencils, crayons, charcoal, chalk, pastels, erasers, markers, styluses, and metals.
